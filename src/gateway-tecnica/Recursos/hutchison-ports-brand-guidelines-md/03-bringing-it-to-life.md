## Web Banners (página 55)

Website banner advertisements for use on third-party websites featuring BU corporate marks must include endorsement straplines when space is available. This does not apply to sponsorship advertisements which only feature the corporate mark itself.

**Corporate Mark Signature:** marca de la Business Unit (ej. "HUTCHISON PORTS THAILAND") + endorsement "A member of CK Hutchison Holdings".

Formatos:
- **Standard banner** (horizontal)

## Branding Our Offices and Buildings (páginas 64–65)

Branding our own infrastructure is a vital step in building a new perception of our brand, particularly amongst our employees.

How we brand our infrastructure will be case-specific for the different kinds of buildings and offices in the network. Branding is applied at **group** and **Business Unit** levels.

- **Corporate Mark on a white background** (nivel grupo).
- **BU Corporate Mark name on a white background** (ej. "HUTCHISON PORTS HIT").

### Edificios más pequeños (página 65)
This is how branding is applied to smaller office buildings, service buildings and depots.

- **Office building:** Logo only with building name, on white background.
- **Service building:** No Corporate Mark or logo, just building name, on white background.
- **Depot:** Typeface painted on a plain surface (ej. número "88" pintado en una pared corrugada).

## Recruitment Advertising (páginas 71–72)

Our recruitment advertising must be visually impactful – and use the design principles of our brand.

> **[Imagen]** Anuncio de revista: "JOIN US. DISCOVER NEW HORIZONS." sobre foto de cielo/mar con shape verde aqua y el Corporate Mark.

### Externo vs interno (página 72)
The two examples show the subtle differences between external and internal recruitment advertising.

The following must be applied to both:
- Corporate Mark
- CK Hutchison endorsement strapline

In terms of size and position, they may vary according to design considerations.

| External communication | Internal communication |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| Inspiring headline message | Factual headline message |
| Less copy - to create interest/intrigue | More copy - to give ample information |
| Two column layout | One column layout |
| Mixing image and shape | Mixing shape and colour |

Ejemplos: externo "JOIN US. DISCOVER NEW HORIZONS." / interno "ONE NETWORK. THOUSANDS OF CAREER MOVES."

## Business Cards (páginas 73–79)

### Summary — 3 opciones (página 73)
- **Option 1:** Front: English / Reverse: Local language.
- **Option 2:** Front: English / Local Language; Reverse: Hutchison Ports logo & website.
- **Option 3:** Front: English / Local Language; Reverse: Hutchison Ports flag & website.

### Group Level (páginas 74–76)
Business cards must be consistent across the organisation – in design and production – to further strengthen our brand. Clarity and legibility are the keywords.

> **Note:** All business cards must be approved by **GCA** before print.

**Especificaciones técnicas (Group Level):**
- **Overall Dimensions:** 90 mm × 56 mm
- **Paper Stock:** Conqueror, CX 22 Diamond White, 350gsm
- **Artwork:** Hutchison Ports Corporate Mark and CK Hutchison endorsement strapline must be reproduced from master artwork.
- **Colour:**
  - Hutchison Ports Corporate Mark: Pantone 294u and 2925u
  - CK Hutchison endorsement strapline: Pantone 425u
  - Text: Pantone 294u
  - Card reverse: Pantone 294u and 2925u
- **Typesetting in English:**
  - Executive's name: 11pt Verlag Bold
  - Executive's title: 7.5pt Verlag Book
  - Company Name: 7.5pt Verlag Bold
  - Address: 7.5pt Verlag Book
  - All leading 8.5pt.

Se muestran las **Options 1–4** del layout (variando líneas de teléfono T/M/F y departamento) más el **Card reverse** (logo centrado + hutchisonports.com).

### Business Unit Level (páginas 77–79)
At Business Unit level, the design includes the **Business Unit Name Corporate Mark** and the content is left aligned.

- El layout permite un máximo de **21 caracteres** para el nombre de la BU. Para excepciones, contactar a GCA directamente.

**Don'ts (con "X" roja):**
- **Don't** use the BU's full name then reduce text size until it fits.
- **Don't** reduce the text and icon proportionally.
- **Don't** make the BU name the same text size as Hutchison Ports.
- **Don't** run the BU name onto two lines.

**Especificaciones (BU Level):** mismas dimensiones (90×56mm), papel (Conqueror CX 22 Diamond White 350gsm), colores (Pantone 294u/2925u/425u) y tipografía que Group Level, pero con BU Corporate Mark.

## Email Signatures (página 92)

To allow for both personal and departmental email accounts, there are **four types of email signatures**. They cater for the inclusion of promotional banners and company addresses.

Company Brand Name

(El nombre de la persona y "Company Brand Name" van en negrita en los cuatro tipos.)

### Email Signatures Application — Option 3 (con Corporate Mark) (página 93)
You may include the corporate mark in the email signature with the height of **38px**. The type sizes shown below should be adhered to for all email signatures.

- **Name / Title / Department Name:** Arial, Regular, 10pt/12pt
- **Company Brand Name + datos de contacto:** Arial, Bold, 10pt/12pt
- **Corporate Mark (BU Name):** 38px de altura (h)
- BU website address (ej. hit.com.hk)

### Email Signatures Application — Option 4 (con banner promocional) (página 94)
The inclusion of a promotional banner is **optional**. When creating one:
- **Maximum width:** 600px. **Recommended minimum height:** 52px.
- Due to the restricted space of these banner heights, se permite una excepción a la regla de clear space del logo (ver Version 1).
- **Maximum height:** 82px. The design style should use the brand elements (ver Version 2).
- Dimensionar el corporate mark y la endorsement strapline según los ejemplos de la página 55.
- Banner message should be confident and clear with a link to the corresponding BU home page.

Versiones:
- **Version 1:** Banner with corporate mark and BU message. Altura 52px (h). (ej. "HUTCHISON PORTS THAILAND — ubi — THE WHOLE PORT IN YOUR POCKET")
- **Version 2:** Banner with Corporate Mark Signature and BU message. Altura 82px (h). Incluye el endoso "A member of CK Hutchison Holdings". (ej. "THE WORLD'S LEADING PORT NETWORK")
