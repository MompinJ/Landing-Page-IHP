# 04. BRAND ARCHITECTURE

> **[Imagen — divisor de sección, página 95]** Fondo amarillo con gran banda diagonal azul ascendente. Número "04" y título "BRAND ARCHITECTURE" en azul marino. Texto introductorio:

The Hutchison Ports group brand overarches our network's individual Business Units. But it doesn't define them.

This section describes how our masterbrand and individual Business Units' brands live side-by-side.

---

## Brand Architecture (página 96)

Each Corporate Mark is specifically designed, from corporate level to Business Unit level.

### Corporate Mark
- **Nivel corporativo:** símbolo (logo) + wordmark "HUTCHISON PORTS".

### Business Unit
La Corporate Mark de Business Unit combina:
- **Logo** (el símbolo)
- **Wordmark** "HUTCHISON PORTS"
- **Business Unit name** (máximo **21 caracteres**), en peso Black bajo el wordmark.

### Examples
- HUTCHISON PORTS — **PORT OF FELIXSTOWE**
- HUTCHISON PORTS — **ICAVE**

## Brand Architecture — Previous vs New Approach (página 97)

Every Business Unit in our network should be identified and branded correctly and consistently. This will help our employees, customers, partners and other audiences understand the shape of our network.

Cuatro casos, comparando el enfoque anterior (Previous) con el nuevo (New):

| # | Caso | Previous Approach | New Approach |
|---|------|-------------------|--------------|
| **1** | **Hutchison Ports Corporate Mark** | HPH corporate mark + CK Hutchison endorsement (logo "HPH") | Hutchison Ports Corporate Mark + CK Hutchison endorsement ("A member of CK Hutchison Holdings") |
| **2** | **Business Unit Corporate Mark (non HPH Trust)** | Business Unit corporate mark + CK Hutchison endorsement (ej. "Port of Felixstowe" antiguo) | Hutchison Ports network mark + Business Unit name + CK Hutchison endorsement (ej. "HUTCHISON PORTS PORT OF FELIXSTOWE") |
| **3** | **HPH Trust** | HPH Trust corporate mark (logo "HPHT") | Hutchison Ports Corporate Mark |
| **4** | **Business Unit Corporate Mark (HPH Trust)** | Business Unit corporate mark + HPH Trust endorsement (ej. "Yantian International Container Terminals", "A member of HPH Trust") | Hutchison Ports network mark + Business Unit name + HPH Trust endorsement (ej. "HUTCHISON PORTS YANTIAN", "A member of HPH Trust") |

## Brand Architecture & Naming Decision Making Tree (página 98)

Hutchison Ports is our brand, we should make it as strong as possible.

Our core business defined as Port BUs only (container and cruise terminals/ports) – and our other operations must strengthen this.

> **When in doubt, please contact GCA for further assistance.**

### Árbol de decisión

**Pregunta inicial: ¿ARE YOU A CONTAINER OR CRUISE TERMINAL/PORT?**

- **YES** → Follow Hutchison Ports brand guidelines and agreed naming and branding principles.
  → Resultado: **HUTCHISON PORTS BU NAME** [Country/City Name/Acronym], approved by **GMD**.

- **NO** → ¿Are you a business/service/product offered to customers outside the Hutchison Ports network?

  - **NO** (servicio dentro de la red). Dos sub-casos:
    - *If you are a service offered only through a specific Hutchison Port to its customers:* To strengthen the Hutchison Ports brand we must attribute all services we offer directly to our BU brands, therefore they should be treated with a service descriptor (e.g. Container Care / ICAVE).
      → Resultado: **HUTCHISON PORTS BU NAME + Service Descriptor**.
    - *If you are a service offered across more than one Hutchison Port to its customers:* Cross port services/initiatives have their own identity but are always endorsed by the brand of the BU that delivers them (e.g. nGen).
      → Resultado: **SUB-BRAND / INTERNAL NAME + BU or Hutchison Ports Corporate Mark**.

  - **YES** (negocio/servicio fuera de la red): You should carry your own brand name and have your own identity and always apply the Hutchison Ports endorsement (e.g. PARIS, Boxreload, Synergy, etc.).
    → Resultado: **BRAND NAME + "A member of Hutchison Ports"**.
